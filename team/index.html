Team

I am a member of Team SAMSON at the MOLTECH-Anjou laboratory, a joint laboratory between CNRS and the Université d’Angers. We are based at the Belle-Beille campus in Angers (France).

Furthermore, I have the pleasure to work with many talented students and post-docs, and with colleagues from other research institutions
